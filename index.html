About

I'm Keri Janschitz — Product Development & Project Lead at Seidra Textilwerke (Draschitz, Carinthia) since 2017, and an AI Content Designer applying that systems-and-operations background to AI-augmented content production since 2025.

The work in this portfolio is anchored in two beliefs that the LOOP brief frames clearly: that AI is a system, not a tool, and that scalable workflows are built by leading with output, not framework decks. Both align with how I have already worked, with and without AI, for years.

The portfolio's four cases are deliberately different shapes. MR.ZHU is a real client deployment with measurable outcome. Audi Vorsprung Local is a spec extension at LOOP-tier scale. AIGrowth Lab is an ongoing public practice of daily experimentation. Munich Fabric Start is the 4-year operational foundation underneath all of it. Together they answer different questions a hiring manager might ask, with different kinds of evidence.
